About

My name is Colleen and I’m a local government nerd (think Leslie Knope) turned software engineer. Coding always fascinated me, but I discovered I wanted to become a software developer when I used a rather clunky accounting software in a previous job. After a few weeks of struggling with the program, I started wondering, “Who built this piece of software? How can I become someone who develops delightful programs people enjoy using?” And thus the coding journey began.

I’m interested in software engineering positions working in and adjacent to the public sector. I believe government agencies offer tremendous support to communities, but need a technological boost to increase access and participation among residences. As a Certified Scrum Master (CSM), I also appreciate agile methodologies and want to build splendid software within a scrum environment.
