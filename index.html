About

Hi, I'm Colleen!

I'm a software engineer and Agile enthusiast with a desire to create and build delightful products that empower people.

Coding always fascinated me, but I didn't think about pursuing software engineering I used a rather clunky accounting software in a previous job. I wondered, “Who built this software? How can I become someone who develops delightful programs people enjoy using?” And thus the coding journey began.

I’m interested in public sector (or adjacent) software engineering positions. I believe government agencies offer community support, but need a technological boost to increase access and participation among residences. As a Certified Scrum Master (CSM), I also appreciate agile methodologies and want to build splendid software within a scrum environment.

I’m also a local government nerd (such as Leslie Knope), Premier League soccer fan, and runner.
